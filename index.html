Data Scientist Vision and Experience

Mr. Junzhi He is currently the data scientist manager of Guavus, a company of Thales, and the project manager of the engineer team part-time.
Junzhi has more than 10 years of software development experience, 3 years of experience as an architect, and 5 years of research experience in machine learning, statistical inference and optimization.
At present, Junzhi focus on how to use machine learning analytic to help traditional and emerging industries achieve higher levels automation, and how to solve the pain points in the implementation of artificial intelligence projects.
